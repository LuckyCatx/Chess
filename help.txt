The following commands are valid at all scope:
	new			create a new board
	help			open help
	credits			show credits
	open <file>		open a .txt or .fen file, extension is necessary


The following commands are valid only when opening an interact-able board:
	filp			flip the board
	put <piece> <square>	put the piece on the square
	remove <square>		remove the piece on the square
	move <square> <square>	move the piece on first square to second square
